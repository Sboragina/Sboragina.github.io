Type 1 Diabetes for Family and Friends

Welcome!

This module is designed for friends and family of adults (young and old) with type 1 diabetes.

Type 1 diabetes is a life-long disease that requires careful management every day. It affects both the person with the disease and their family and friends. When a person with type 1 diabetes receives positive support from their family and friends, they experience better health outcomes and practise better self-management of their diabetes. Their family and friends benefit, too.

In this module, we'll look at the kinds of support you can offer a person with type 1 diabetes, including what you can do in the case of a diabetic emergency.
