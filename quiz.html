  1. People with type 1 diabetes

    Feedback: Option a is correct. The treatment of diabetes depends on whether a person has type 1 diabetes or type 2 diabetes. People with type 1 diabetes must inject insulin into their bodies multiple times a day to manage their blood sugar levels. Their bodies do not produce sufficient insulin for them to survive. Without injecting insulin, they will die. Those with type 2 diabetes may take pills and/or insulin to manage their blood sugar levels; some may be able to reverse it by improving their diet and activity levels.

    Feedback: Option b is best. Different diets may work best for different people. Sharing information may be helpful if you present it as an option to consider and not a cure or solution.
