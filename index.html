Introduction

Welcome! This module is for friends and family of adults (young and old) with type 1 diabetes.

Type 1 diabetes is a life-long disease that requires careful management every day. It affects both the person with the disease and their family and friends.

A person with type 1 diabetes who receives positive social support experiences better health outcomes and better manages their diabetes. Their family and friends benefit, too.

Learning Goals

By the end of this module, you will know the difference between type 1 diabetes and type 2 diabetes, how you can support a person with type 1 diabetes, and what you can do in the case of a diabetic emergency.

Module Structure

The module should take about one hour to complete.

As you explore the topics below, you'll hear from people with type 1 diabetes. They'll describe challenges they've faced and times they felt supported (or not) by those around them.

Bridget

Each topic ends with a prompt you can use when talking with someone who has type 1 diabetes to find out more about the challenges they encounter and how best to support them.
