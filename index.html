1. Type 1 Diabetes:
Guide for Family and Friends

Introduction

Welcome! This module is designed for friends and family of adults (young and old) with type 1 diabetes.

Type 1 diabetes is a life-long disease that requires careful management every day. It affects both the person with the disease and their family and friends. When a person with type 1 diabetes receives positive social support, they experience better health outcomes and practise better self-management of their diabetes. Their family and friends benefit, too.

Learning Goals

By the end of this module, you will be aware of the kinds of support you can offer a person with type 1 diabetes, and you will know what to do in the case of a diabetic emergency.

Module Structure

As you explore the topics below, you'll hear from those with type 1 diabetes as they describe challenges they face and times they felt supported (or not) by those around them.

At the end of each topic is a prompt for you to discuss with the person you know with type 1 diabetes, to find out what challenges they encounter and how they prefer to be supported.

The module should take about one hour to complete.
